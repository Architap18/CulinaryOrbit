Recipe Manager - FAQ
1. What is the Recipe Manager? ⌄
It is a tool where you can save, organize, and view all your cooking recipes in one place.
2. How do I add a recipe? ⌄
Go to Add Recipe, fill in the details, and click Save.
3. Can I edit or delete a recipe? ⌄
Yes. Open any recipe and choose Edit or Delete.
4. How do I search for a recipe? ⌄
Use the search bar to find recipes by name, ingredients, or tags.
5. Can I categorize my recipes? ⌄
Yes. You can add tags like breakfast, dinner, dessert, etc.
6. Can I add images to a recipe? ⌄
Yes, you can upload one or more images for each recipe.
7. How do I share a recipe? ⌄
You can share using a link or download it as a PDF (depending on your app version).
8. Does the Recipe Manager work offline? ⌄
Yes. All saved recipes can be accessed offline.
9. Is my data backed up? ⌄
If backup is enabled, your recipes will be saved to cloud storage automatically.
10. Do I need an account to use it? ⌄
No. You can use it without an account, but backup and sync need an account.
11. Who can I contact for help? ⌄
You can reach out through the app’s Support section.
